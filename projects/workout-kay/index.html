02

Push Day — Upper Body Bias

Pressing strength. Core-loaded lower body. Get after it.

⏱ 0:00
🔥

Warm-Up

Prime the shoulders and core before pressing.

Ankle CARS

2 each ankle

Cue: Full circles in both directions. Slow and controlled. Feel every position in the rotation.

Push-Up to Downward Dog

2 × 5

Cue: Do a push-up, then press your hips up into a pike stretch. Feel the hamstrings and shoulders open. Come back down, repeat.

Split Squat (Iso Hold)

2 × 20s each leg

Cue: Hold the bottom of the split squat. Feel the stretch in the back hip flexor. Tall chest, ribs down.

Dead Bug

2 × 8 each side

Cue: Low back glued to the floor. Extend opposite arm and leg slowly. The moment your back lifts, stop. Breathe through it.

Scapula Push-Ups

2 × 10

Cue: Push-up position, arms locked. Let your chest sink between your shoulder blades, then push the ground away. No elbow bend. Feel the muscles on the side of your ribs.

Lateral Skater Jumps

3 × 5 each side

Cue: Jump sideways, land on one foot, STICK IT. Hold 2 seconds. Light and quick. Quiet landing.

💪

Main Work

Superset A

DB Bench Press

3 × 10

Cue: Control down for 3 seconds, press up with control. Shoulder blades squeezed together the whole time. Don't bounce at the bottom.

Step-Up (Offset — Single DB)

3 × 8 each leg

Cue: One DB in one hand (suitcase hold). Step up, drive through the top foot to full hip extension. Your core is fighting the offset — stay tall, don't lean.

Superset B

Half-Kneeling DB Press

3 × 8 each arm

Cue: One knee down, same-side arm presses. Core stays locked — no leaning, no arching. The press starts from the brace.

Walking Lunge (Hands Overhead)

3 × 8 each leg

Cue: Bodyweight, hands locked straight overhead. Every step your core has to fight to keep the ribs down while the arms stay up. That's the core work.

⚡

Accessory

Giant Set C

Cable Tricep Pushdown

2 × 15

Cue: Elbows pinned to your sides. Full lockout at the bottom, slow back up. Light weight, feel the squeeze.

Lateral DB Fly

2 × 12

Cue: Light weight. Lift the DBs out to the sides to shoulder height. Control the lower. Don't use momentum.

Side Plank

2 × 20s each side

Cue: Elbow under shoulder, hips up, squeeze the bottom glute. Breathe. If your hips drop, the set is over.

🏁

Finisher

Plank (Swiss Ball)

2 × 30s

Cue: Forearms on the ball. Squeeze everything — glutes, abs, quads. Breathe through the brace. The ball wants to move. Don't let it.

03

Pull Day — Upper Body Bias

Build the back. Core-loaded lower body. Strong from behind.

⏱ 0:00
🔥

Warm-Up

Activate the back and get the hips moving.

Wrist CARS

2 each wrist

Cue: Make a fist. Full circles in both directions, then flip palm up and down slowly.

Prone Y-T-W

1 × 8 each position

Cue: Face down. Y = arms overhead thumbs up. T = arms out to sides. W = elbows bent, rotate hands up. Light, slow, squeeze at the top.

Bodyweight Squat (Tempo)

2 × 8

Cue: 4 seconds down, 1 second up. Go as deep as you can with a tall chest. This is about control, not speed.

Bear Crawl (Static Hold)

2 × 20s

Cue: Hands under shoulders, knees under hips. Lift knees 1 inch off the ground. Hold. Breathe. If you're shaking, good.

Single-Leg Balance (Short Foot)

2 × 20s each foot

Cue: Stand on one foot. Pull the ball of your foot back toward your heel to build the arch. Hold it. Eyes forward, breathe.

Max Height Skips

3 × 5 each leg

Cue: Skip as HIGH as you can. Drive the knee up, fire the opposite arm. Be explosive. Land soft.

💪

Main Work

Superset A

Single-Arm DB Row (Bench-Supported)

3 × 10 each arm

Cue: One knee and hand on the bench. Row the DB to your hip, squeeze the shoulder blade at the top. Go heavier here — this is your primary pull.

B-Stance Hip Thrust

3 × 10 each side

Cue: Back on bench, one foot plants and drives, other heel rests lightly for balance. Drive hips to full lockout. Squeeze the glute HARD at the top.

Superset B

Lat Pulldown

3 × 12

Cue: Pull your shoulder blades DOWN first, then bend the elbows and pull the bar to your upper chest. Control the return. No swinging.

Single-Leg RDL — DB (Contralateral)

3 × 8 each leg

Cue: DB in the opposite hand from the standing leg. Hinge forward, reach the free leg back. The DB pulls you into rotation — resist it. Your core is working as hard as your hamstring.

⚡

Accessory

Giant Set C

Cable Row (Seated, Single-Arm)

2 × 12 each arm

Cue: One arm at a time. Squeeze the shoulder blade back, control the return. Constant tension the whole time.

Face Pull (Cable)

2 × 15

Cue: Pull to face, elbows high, rotate hands out at the end. Rear delts and rotator cuff. This is daily maintenance for healthy shoulders.

DB Bicep Curl

2 × 12

Cue: No swinging. Elbows pinned. Slow up, slower down. Strong biceps protect the elbows.

🏁

Finisher

Farmer Carry (Heavy)

2 × 40yd

Cue: Heavy DBs or KBs, both hands. Walk tall, squeeze the handles, own every step. Grip, traps, core — everything works.

04

Push Day — Lower Body Bias

Squat patterns. Core-loaded pressing. Finish strong.

⏱ 0:00
🔥

Warm-Up

Open the hips, brace the core, prime the legs.

Hip CARS (Quadruped)

2 each hip

Cue: Hands and knees. Lift the knee out to the side, rotate it through the biggest circle you can, reverse. Slow and controlled.

90/90 Stretch (Passive Hold)

2 × 30s each side

Cue: Sit with both legs at 90 degrees. Lean into the front hip. Breathe and sink into it. Don't force it — just let gravity work.

Glute Bridge (Single-Leg Tap)

2 × 8 each side

Cue: Bridge up on both feet, then lift one foot an inch off the ground, tap it back down. The glute on the planted side has to hold you up.

Bird-Dog

2 × 8 each side

Cue: Opposite arm and leg extend at the same time. Slow. Don't let your hips shift or your back arch. Core stability from the ground up.

Push-Up (Tempo)

2 × 5

Cue: 4 seconds down, 1 second up. Quality over quantity. Core stays tight the whole time.

Squat Jump to Stick

3 × 3

Cue: Squat down, jump up, land soft, FREEZE for 3 seconds. Quiet feet. The landing is the test.

💪

Main Work

Superset A

Goblet Squat (Heel Elevated)

3 × 10

Cue: Heels on a plate or wedge. KB at chest. 3 seconds down, go as deep as your mobility allows. Tall chest, ribs down.

Single-Arm DB Bench Press

3 × 10 each arm

Cue: One DB, full bench. Your core has to fight to keep you from rolling off — that's the point. Press up, control down. Squeeze everything.

Superset B

Bulgarian Split Squat (Iso Hold → Reps)

3 × 6 each leg

Cue: Rear foot on bench. Set 1: hold the bottom for 15 seconds. Sets 2-3: slow reps. This is new and it's supposed to be hard. Own the position before you rush through it.

Landmine Press (Iso Lunge Stance)

3 × 8 each arm

Cue: Hold a lunge, press the landmine with one arm. Your legs hold the position while your arm does the work. Core is fighting everything — that's the design.

⚡

Accessory

Superset C

Swiss Ball Hamstring Curl

2 × 10

Cue: Heels on the ball, hips up. Curl the ball toward your butt, extend back out. Hips stay up the entire time — if they drop, you lost it.

Cable/Band Chest Fly

2 × 12

Cue: Light weight. Arms slightly bent, bring the handles together in front of your chest. Squeeze. Control the return.

🏁

Finisher

Cross-Body Carry (Rack + Suitcase)

2 × 30yd each setup

Cue: One KB in rack (at your shoulder), one DB at your side. Walk tall. Two different forces pulling you in two directions — your core solves the puzzle. Swap sides, repeat.
