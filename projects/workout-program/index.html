The Big 3 Checklist

Master these three fundamentals. Every session starts here.

Foot Position / Mechanics

Short Foot Activation

3 sets · 10 reps each foot · 5s hold

Cue: Pull the ball of your foot toward your heel without curling toes. Build your arch.

▶ Watch video

Toe Yoga (Splay & Isolate)

2 sets · 8 reps · Big toe up / little toes down, then switch

Cue: Control each toe independently. Slow is the goal.

▶ Watch video

Tripod Foot Stance Balance

2 sets · 30s each foot

Cue: Weight even across big toe, little toe, and heel. Stay stacked.

▶ Watch video

Bracing / Breathing

Dead Bug (Rib Cage Lock)

3 sets · 8 reps each side

Cue: Ribs stay down. Low back stays flat. Move slow.

▶ Watch video

90/90 Hip Lift with Balloon (or exhale)

2 sets · 5 breaths

Cue: Hamstrings on. Exhale fully to drop the ribs. Feel the abs lock in.

▶ Watch video

Bear Crawl Hold (Knees 1" Off Ground)

3 sets · 20s hold

Cue: Brace like someone's about to hit you. Breathe through the brace.

▶ Watch video

Crocodile Breathing (Prone)

2 sets · 8 breaths

Cue: Belly pushes into the floor on inhale. Expand into the sides and back. Slow exhale.

▶ Watch video

360 Breathing (Seated, Band Around Ribs)

2 sets · 8 breaths

Cue: Breathe into the band in all directions -- front, sides, and back. No chest rise.

▶ Watch video

Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)

1 set · 2 minutes

Cue: 4s inhale, 4s hold, 4s exhale, 4s hold. Calm the system down.

▶ Watch video
💪

Neck / Shoulder Position

Chin Tuck (Deep Neck Flexor Activation)

3 sets · 10 reps · 5s hold

Cue: Make a double chin. Pull the back of your skull straight back. Keep eyes level.

▶ Watch video

Wall Slide (Scapular Upward Rotation)

2 sets · 10 reps

Cue: Back flat on wall. Elbows and wrists stay in contact. Slide up slow, squeeze at top.

▶ Watch video

Prone Y-T-W Raises

2 sets · 8 reps each position

Cue: Thumbs up, squeeze shoulder blades. Low trap and mid trap activation. No shrugging.

▶ Watch video
