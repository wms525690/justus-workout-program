The Big 3 Checklist

Master these three fundamentals. Every session starts here.

Foot Position / Mechanics

Short Foot Activation → Balance Drill

3 sets · Build the arch seated, then stand and hold single-leg · Log time per set

Cue: Pull ball of foot toward heel without curling toes -- build the arch. Once you feel it, stand on that foot. Let the arch engagement gently spiral your knee and femur outward into external rotation. This is the alignment that protects the ACL. Hold as long as you can maintain the arch and the rotation.

Toe Yoga (Splay & Isolate)

2 sets · 8 reps · Big toe up / little toes down, then switch

Cue: Control each toe independently. Slow is the goal.

▶ Watch video

Ball-of-Foot Balance (Short Foot + ER)

3 sets each foot · Log time per set

Cue: Rise onto the balls of your feet, heels off the ground. Maintain the short foot arch -- don't let it collapse. Feel the knee and femur gently rotate outward from the foot's position. The foot dictates whether the knee is protected or compromised. Hold as long as form holds.

Bracing / Breathing

360 Breathing

3 sets · 8 breaths per set

Cue: Seated or supine. Inhale through the nose and expand into ALL directions -- front, sides, and back. Ribs should expand laterally, not rise up. Exhale fully and feel the ribs button down toward the belly button. No chest rise. This is your brace foundation.

▶ Watch video

Dead Bug (Static → Movement)

4 sets · Set 1: static hold, lock position · Sets 2-4: slow contralateral movement · Log reps per set

Cue: Ribs buttoned down, low back flat on the floor. Set 1 -- just hold the position and breathe through your brace. Own it before you move. Sets 2-4 -- slow opposite arm and leg extension. The moment your back arches off the floor, the set is over. Initiate every rep from the exhale, from the core out.

▶ Watch video

Bear Crawl (Static Hold → Crawl)

4 sets · Set 1: static hold, knees 1" off ground · Sets 2-4: slow controlled crawling · Log time per set

Cue: Set 1 -- tuck toes, lift knees 1 inch. Hold. Breathe through the brace. If you're shaking, good. Sets 2-4 -- opposite hand and foot move together, slow and controlled. A cup of water on your back should not spill. Synchronize the breath with each step. Nasal breathing only -- if you have to mouth-breathe, slow down.

Neck / Shoulder Position

Chin Tuck (Deep Neck Flexor Activation)

3 sets · 10 reps · 5s hold

Cue: Make a double chin. Pull the back of your skull straight back. Keep eyes level.

▶ Watch video

Wall Slide (Scapular Upward Rotation)

2 sets · 10 reps

Cue: Back flat on wall, chin tucked, eyes level. Elbows and wrists stay in contact. Slide up slow, squeeze at top. Maintain the chin tuck the entire time -- if the head pushes forward, you've lost the neck position.
